Pixie Hollow

Background

One of my earliest memories of the internet was playing the Disney Fairies game called Pixie Hollow. The site had several mini-games and you could navigate the world as your own fairy avatar. There were several types of fairies you could choose from.

Some types were water, garden, and light.

Mini-Games

Pixie Hollow included many mini-games that you could play by collecting items from around the game's environment. These items would be store in your "pouch."

Movies

The Pixie Hollow online game was part of a franchise that included movies, and those were also part of my childhood memories.
